As we march towards a new age of sustainability, what will come of the gas staitons of today? One suggestion is that they can simply be transformed into supercharging stations, but, car manufacturers have already started to build their own, which will be an extra source of income, aside from the car sales.

Gas stations aren't simply a place where people fill up their tanks, we also have to consider the people who work their, and the surrounding businesses that rely on those stations. This is especially true in the trucking industry.
